Welcome.

I post things here that I make, discover, or find interesting.

Like the blog’s title suggests, there isn’t really a theme to any of the content here. Have a browse and see if anything piques your fancy.

I plan on posting extremely irregularly, focusing on things I’ve been working on outside of my day-to-day gamedev job. Expect a range of topics, from bagel recipes to ESP32-based hydroponic controllers.

Hope you find something cool! Feel free to flick me an email or post a comment.

— Francis
